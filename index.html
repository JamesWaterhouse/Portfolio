About Me

I’m a recent graduate from the Mayden Academy Full Stack Programme, an intensive 16 week course in my home town, Bath.

Working as part of a small team, we developed projects using technologies such as PHP, SQL, Slim and JavaScript. An emphasis was that projects should follow SOLID principles and be comprehensively unit-tested.

Each project was built using an agile framework, and during the course I gained Certified Scrum Master status.

I’m keen to gain a deeper understanding of languages already explored, as well as learning new concepts and information along the way. With a fairly varied basic knowledge, I'm excited to develop into a more specialised field. Whilst back-end technologies are my focus, the full stack training has given me a good set of frontend skills.

My primary interest is using technology for social good, or common benefit, whether in an enterprise, community, or charitable setting. I aspire to be part of a team bringing tangible advantage to society, at every stage of my career.
