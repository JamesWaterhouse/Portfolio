About Me

I’m a recent graduate from the Mayden Academy Full Stack Programme, an intensive 16 week course. Each project was developed within a small team, using an agile framework, and during the course I gained certified Scrum master status. I’m keen to gain a deeper understanding of languages already explored, as well as learning new concepts and information along the way. With a fairly varied basic knowledge, I'm excited to develop into a more specialised field. My primary interest is using technology for social good, whether in an enterprise, community, or charitable setting. I aspire to be part of a team bringing tangible benefit to society, at every stage of my career.
